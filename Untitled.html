    How I use the table, and what columns I look at.

    You have to look at everything collectively. The columns I mostly look at are ProjMins, Useage%, OvP, ProjTeamScore, Gameline, SalaryAdjust, RecentPerformance, PtsvsPos, and CriteriaRanking. After that I find the best combo of players and throw them in a line. The key to using this table is Trust the Process and HAVE NO BIAS OPINION!


    ProjMins

    ProjMins: How many minutes a player is projected to play


    Useage%

    Useage%: How long a player has control of the ball per possession (Anything above 30% (varies based on position) is in the clear. It is ok to go lower if other categories are good)


    PPG/L10

    PPG/L10: Average fantasy points per game for the last 10 games


    PPM(Points per Minute)

    PPM(Points per Minute): How many fantasy points a player gets per minute (Anything above 1.2 is in the clear. It is ok to go lower if other categories are good)


    OVP(Offense Vs Position)

    OVP(Offense Vs Position): How well the opponent defends that specific position (Anything above 1.0 is in the clear. It is ok to go lower if other categories are good)


    ProjTeamScore

    ProjTeamScore: Projection of the Total team points


    GameLine

    GameLine: Projection of the Total game points


    ProjectedPts

    ProjectedPts: My Projection of points a specific player will score.


    Value

    Value: Projected value of a specific player based on projections (Try to stay above 5.0 but it is ok to go below if other categories are good)


    SalaryAdjust

    SalaryAdjust: Their last game salary compared to their current salary (Anything 400 and below is in the clear. Above 400 is a red flag. - is equivalent to a 0 which is great)


    RecentPerformance

    RecentPerformance: Their last game compared to their average (Anything 10 and below is in the clear. Above 10 is a red flag. - is equivalent to a 0 which is great)


    PtsVPos(Points vs Position)

    PtsVPos(Points vs Position): Points allowed by the defensive position


    CriteriaRanking

    CriteriaRanking: The rank I rank players. 7-6 and 5 are good to go but anything below 5 is a red flag
